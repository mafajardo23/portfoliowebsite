About

Hi! I’m Maria Fajardo — born and raised in Colombia and currently studying Computer Science and Digital Studies at Davidson College in North Carolina.

I’ve always been a curious and creative person. I loved painting and designing. Growing up, I found joy in baking and even turned that passion into a small business. As the business grew, I had a need for a website. This led me to explore the intersection of technology, design, and human behavior.

Today, I’m passionate about creating digital experiences that are thoughtful, user-centered, and socially aware. Whether I’m designing interfaces, coding a project, or doing user research, I bring both my analytical and creative sides to the table.

Outside of tech, I still bake (a lot), appreciate a good sunset, and enjoy listening to people’s stories — because I believe the best designs start by paying attention.
